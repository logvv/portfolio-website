About

Product development with a buyer-focused mindset.

I focus on product development and product planning for overseas home and lifestyle markets, with experience across home goods, kitchen and tableware, storage, seasonal gifts, candles, kids items, and promotional products.

My work connects trend direction, customer needs, price-positioning, material and color planning, collection logic, and presentation storytelling—helping ideas move from research to commercially clear product proposals.
